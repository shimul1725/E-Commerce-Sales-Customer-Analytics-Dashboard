Project Title
E-Commerce Sales & Customer Analytics Dashboard
Overview

This project demonstrates the use of Power BI for business intelligence, KPI reporting, customer analytics, and sales performance monitoring.

The dashboard was built using sales transaction data and provides actionable insights for decision-makers.

Objectives
Analyze sales performance
Track business KPIs
Identify top-performing products
Monitor regional sales performance
Segment customers based on purchasing behavior
Generate business insights for management

Tools Used

Product Analytics

Product Performance Analysis

Key Insights
The electronics category generated the highest revenue.
A small group of customers contributed a significant share of total sales.
Regional performance varied significantly across locations.
Product profitability differed across categories.

Business Recommendations
Focus marketing efforts on high-performing categories.
Develop retention strategies for high-value customers.
Improve sales performance in low-performing regions.
Monitor product profitability regularly.
